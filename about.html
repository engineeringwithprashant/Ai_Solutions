Our Story

A simple idea — that AI should work for everyone, reliably

How we turned one conviction into a company

Sunderland, 2019 — the belief that AI should never fail in production

AI-Solutions was founded in Sunderland, UK, with a clear conviction: AI systems must be reliable, transparent, and safe at scale. What began as a small team of machine learning engineers has grown into a specialist AI firm trusted by over 157 clients across five industries.

We started with a single insight: too many organisations were building AI that looked impressive in demos but crumbled under production conditions. The datasets weren't ready. The models weren't monitored. The human oversight wasn't there. That became the conviction we built this company on.

Over the years, AI-Solutions perfected the process of building production-ready AI — combining deep technical expertise with a culture of rigorous quality and responsible deployment.

Explore our services

Our roots are strong, but growth demands change

Building on a strong foundation, we're expanding beyond traditional AI deployment services to support the entire AI lifecycle — from raw data to real-time decision-making at scale.

No matter how much an ML team fine-tunes their models or enhances training datasets, if the underlying data isn't trusted and the model isn't monitored, performance will degrade.

This is where our story takes a pivotal turn.

We're now a full-cycle AI company. We help businesses combine machine intelligence and human expertise to build AI that's not only accurate — but safe, explainable, and continuously improving. Our platform's human-in-the-loop methodology corrects errors and edge cases that automation alone can't handle.
